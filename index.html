About Me

Welcome everybody!
My name is Anne Shirley (Anne with and "e"),
I live in the wonderful Avonelea on Prince Edward Island. The Cuthbert brothers, Marilla and Matthew are my family, and Diana Berry is my best friend. I love dreaming wide eyed, telling stories and wandering in the nature asking myself too many questions about all that constantly wonder me. Isn't life incredible?
